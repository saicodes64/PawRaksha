About PawRaksha

PawRaksha is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to help pet owners manage their pet's health and wellness. Our system provides breed-specific vaccination reminders, pet care alerts, and an integrated IoT-based pet feeder for remote feeding control. By combining technology with pet care, ensures pets receive timely healthcare, improving their well-being while making pet management effortless for owners.
